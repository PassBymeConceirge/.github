# Pass By Me — Concierge End-of-Life Consulting

Official website and digital infrastructure for **Pass By Me**, a concierge end-of-life consulting service dedicated to helping individuals and families navigate hospice care, planning, and compassionate support during one of life’s most important transitions.

This project establishes the foundational digital presence for the business, including domain setup, email configuration, and a professional website designed to build trust and allow clients to easily connect with services.

---

# Project Overview

**Pass By Me** provides concierge-style consulting services that assist families with navigating the complex emotional and logistical challenges surrounding end-of-life planning.

The website serves as the primary public-facing platform for the business and will provide:

- Information about services
- Background on the mission and approach
- Consultation scheduling
- Direct contact for families seeking support

The goal of this project is to create a calm, compassionate, and professional digital experience that reflects the sensitive nature of the services offered.

---

# Project Goals

The primary objectives for this project include:

• Establish a professional online presence  
• Provide clear and compassionate messaging  
• Enable potential clients to easily request consultations  
• Build trust through thoughtful design and content  
• Create a scalable foundation for future operational tools

---

# Phase 1 – Initial Launch (May 15)

The first phase focuses on launching the essential infrastructure needed for the business to begin operating.

### Deliverables

- Domain name registration and configuration
- Professional email setup
- Website design and development
- Contact and consultation request system
- Basic SEO setup
- Secure hosting and deployment

### Core Website Pages

- **Home** – Introduction to Pass By Me and its mission  
- **About** – Background, philosophy, and purpose of the service  
- **Services** – Overview of concierge end-of-life consulting offerings  
- **Contact** – Contact form and direct communication options  
- **Consultation** – Scheduling and consultation request functionality
